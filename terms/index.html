About

What exactly are an app? To us is an app a plugin for our Actionpad framework LIME Bootstrap. An app are not developed as a part of LIME Pro and thus is not developed by our development team. An app is in most case developed to solve a specific need or during some of our Appathon(hackathon).

All apps that you can find in the appstore are developed to be used in a LIME Pro database with LIME Bootstrap installed. Are you missing LIME Bootstrap or need any help with an app, contact your sales rep or any other employee at Lundalogik.

All apps that isn't flagged with "paid" are free to download and use. It's also free to modify this and further develop the apps in the appstore.

Apps downloaded from the appstore are not included in Lundalogiks ordinary service agreement.
